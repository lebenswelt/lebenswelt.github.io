A New Website; A New Vector For Introducing Error

A year is a long time for a small technology business. In the twelve months from July 15, 2017 to July 15, 2018 EmptyRespon.se has changed homes, business practices (both financial and ethical) and now its web presence.

June of last year we announced we would be trying new things and we truly have. We tried expanding our staff to three engineers, we tried giving away all our profit, we tried to run a handshake-based business from an hour and a half away.

You can probably guess which one of those was a success.

Our failures:

EmptyRespon.se saw a regrettable reduction in clients as we moved our operation a little farther west. Our most loyal clients remain and EmptyRespon.se is still able to cover costs but our ability to give away money and start overly ambitious projects has decidedly dropped.

Our success:

Taking this as a chance to capitalize on what we do well, EmptyRespon.se is now transitioning away from repair and support for individuals to focus on providing concierge support services for small businesses. We are absolutely still helping individuals but our waivers have changed and we will be seeing significantly fewer clients as we spend more and more time taking the headache out of small business technology.

Another lesson this transition taught us: engineers apparently enjoy being paid to solve problems. Asking potential employees to be altruistic was met with skepticism. Asking them to take an aescetic's pay scale proved too much. So, for now and a while longer, everyone's stuck with me, my bots, and our favorite intern Nick.

Don't be disheartened, though. While all our timetables have been pushed back per funding and personnel allocation, nothing is cancelled and something totally different is still on the way.

We hope you continue to enjoy EmptyRespon.se (revision 2.0) for the coming year as we make more changes, try new things, and hopefully build something we can all marvel at.
