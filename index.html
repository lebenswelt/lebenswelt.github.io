About EmptyRespon.se

Affordability Oriented

Unlike traditional businesses, EmptyRespon.se has no desire to amass a vast horde of wealth. Our primary metrics for success are client education and happiness with billing existing solely to offset entropy and the costs of experimentation.

Whimsy-Fueled Innovation

EmptyRespon.se values the creativity and abiity of its engineers above all. This non-traditional business model allows us to be as flexible, helpful, and unique as possible.

Space-Tested Technology

With satellite launches, shoddy work literally falls out of the sky. EmptyRespon.se was founded by space-science engineer Eric Lawson to bring star-bound solutions to real-world problems.

Data-Driven Results

In its first year, EmptyRespon.se saved its customers 27 thousand dollars in software and hardware licensing and technical support fees. Our clients appreciate the saved revenue and we appreciate those dollars not supporting shady computing business practices. Last year, EmptyRespon.se was fortunate enough to donate 83 percent of our total profit to deserving charitable organizations.
